Infix- its the usual way of doing things
    Pemdas: (human readable)
        parenthesis ()[]{}
        exponent [right to left]
        multiplication and division [left to right]
        addition and subtraction [left to right]

Prefix (polish notation) (good for machines)
    <operator><operand><operand>

Postfix (reverse polish notation) (good for machines)
    <operand><operand><operator>

infix      Prefix     Postfix
2 + 3       + 2 3      2 3 +
a+*c        +a*bc      abc*+
